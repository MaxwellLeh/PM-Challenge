How to Play

  1. Register/Login: Create a new account or log in with your existing account.
  2. Select a country: Choose a country from the interactive map
  3. Answer Questions: Read each question carefully and select the correct answer.
  4. Submit Answers: Submit your answers to get your score and see your rank.
  5. Check Rank: Based on your score, you will be given a rank from Town Councillor to President.
  6. Improve Your Rank: Continue playing and improving your score to achieve higher ranks.

Rankings

  • 0-10 Points: Town Councillor - You’ve just started your journey in understanding geography. As a Town Councillor, you’re responsible for small local decisions. Keep learning to advance your rank!
  • 11-20 Points: Mayor - You’ve got a solid grasp of some countries and capitals. As a Mayor, you’re now making decisions that affect a larger community. Aim higher to expand your influence!
  • 21-30 Points: Governor - Your knowledge is impressive! As a Governor, you oversee a significant region, making important decisions. Keep going to reach the top tiers of leadership!
  • 31-40 Points: Minister - You’re one of the top leaders now! As a Minister, you’re involved in national governance, making crucial decisions for your country. Only a few points away from ultimate leadership!
  • 41-49 Points: Prime Minister - Exceptional work! As Prime Minister, you’re the head of government, leading the nation with your vast knowledge. Just one step away from the pinnacle of power!
  • 50 Points: President - Outstanding! You’ve achieved the highest rank—President! Your knowledge of countries and capitals is unparalleled. You now stand as a global leader in geography!
